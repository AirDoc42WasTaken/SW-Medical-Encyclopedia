Starting in 89 VE the Abyssal Virus was spread by Wyrma. The virus was quickly eradicated through the combined efforts of the SPD, SSH, and other groups.

Spread:

A86 is spread through contact with infected.

Prevention:

Social distance from those infected and wear a mask if possible.

Symptoms:

  • Severe headaches
  • Hallucinations
  • Dizziness
  • Sweaty and swelled hands
  • Red ears

Cure:

Drink one Dryads Forgiveness.
